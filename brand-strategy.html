What Brand Strategy Actually Is
Before a Word is Written.
Before a Frame is Shot.
Most people think strategy is a document. It is not. It is a decision about what to say, what to leave out, and who you are actually talking to. Get this wrong and no amount of beautiful video will save you. Get it right and even a bad execution will find its audience.
01 · Positioning
Where does this brand sit in the world? Not just versus competitors, but in the mind of the person it needs to reach.
02 · Audience
Not demographics. Desires. What does this person want to feel? What are they afraid of? What makes them trust a brand?
03 · Voice
How does the brand speak? What words does it never use? Tone is not decoration. It is identity.
04 · The Idea
The one thing the brand stands for. Simple enough to say in a sentence. Strong enough to build everything else on top of.
